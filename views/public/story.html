Wikmemkong Unceded Territory is the sort of quiet native community one would expect on an island. As the only place in Canada that is not controlled by whites, Manitoulin Island is pristine in history, place and spirit. It is here that Austin Pangowish spent all his life, with the exception of one trip to Chicago for a concert. He has never travelled outside of the island. Young, and yet with a zeal to share his thoughts and culture, he has a lot of hope for his culture and people. He plans to finish high school and study plant biology. He says he needs to preserve his culture.
