ABOUT THIS PROJECT: A series of living-portraits

The Moth connects us, as
humans. Because we all
have stories. Or perhaps, because
we are, as humans, already an
assemblage of stories. And the gulf
that exists between us as people is that
when we look at each other we might
see faces, skin color, gender, race, or
attitudes, but we don't see, we can't see,
the stories. And once we hear each
other's stories we realize that the
things we see as dividing us are,
all too often, illusions.

Neil Gaiman
